Drums

Drums may appear to others to be nothing more than objects to be beaten with a stick, yet they are much more to some of us. Without someone thumping on the drum set, for example, no band is complete. Drummers may appear to be merely background players on set, but if you pay attention to the music, you’ll notice that without them, the song isn’t nearly as good; it lacks the same vitality. Drums are in fact the heart of a song. Drums have the ability to get people up and moving, bouncing their bodies to the beat of the fun and excitement.
